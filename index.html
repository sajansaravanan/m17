Jupiter - The Giant Planet

Jupiter is the largest planet in our Solar System, known for its thick atmosphere and Great Red Spot.

This gas giant is primarily composed of hydrogen and helium, and has a powerful magnetic field.

Jupiter is famous for its many moons, including Ganymede, the largest moon in the Solar System.

Interesting Facts

Jupiter has a faint ring system made up of dust particles.

The Great Red Spot is a giant storm larger than Earth, raging for hundreds of years.

Jupiter rotates on its axis every 9.9 hours, making it the fastest spinning planet.
